# 🍽️ Impact of Eating Out on Health (Power BI Project)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes how **eating out frequency** impacts key health indicators such as **BMI, cholesterol, blood pressure, diabetes, and overall health score**.
The dashboard provides actionable insights into lifestyle patterns and their effects on health.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze the relationship between eating habits and health metrics

* Identify high-risk groups based on lifestyle patterns

* Provide insights for healthier living decisions

## 📂 Dataset Details

The dataset contains **200 records** with the following fields:

* **Person_ID** – Unique identifier

* **Age** – Age of individuals (18–60)

* **Gender** – Male/Female

* **Occupation** – Student, Employee, Business, Freelancer

* **Eat_Out_Frequency** – Times per week (0–7)

* **Preferred_EatOut_Type** – Fast Food, Casual Dining, Street Food, Healthy Café

* **Daily_Calories** – 1600–3500 kcal

* **Exercise_Hours_Per_Week** – 0–10 hours

* **Sleep_Hours** – 4–9 hours

* **BMI** – Body Mass Index

* **BloodPressure** – Normal, PreHypertension, Hypertension

* **Cholesterol_Level** – Normal, Borderline, High

* **Diabetes** – Yes/No

* **Health_Score** – 20–95 scale

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Data visualization

* **Power Query** – Data cleaning & transformation

* **DAX** – Measures & calculations


## 🧹 Data Cleaning Steps

* Handled missing/null values

* Standardized categorical values (Yes/No, Blood Pressure levels)

* Ensured correct data types (numeric vs categorical)

* Removed inconsistencies (extra spaces, case issues)


## 📈 Dashboard Pages

### 🟢 1. Overview Dashboard

* KPI Cards:

  * Avg Eat-Out Frequency

  * Avg Health Score

  * % Diabetes

  * % Hypertension

* Filters: Gender, Occupation


### 🔵 2. Eating Out vs Health

* Bar Chart → Eat-Out Frequency vs Avg BMI

* Line Chart → Eat-Out Frequency vs Health Score

* Scatter Plot → Calories vs Health Score (Size = Exercise Hours)


### 🟣 3. Lifestyle & Demographics

* Stacked Column → Eat-Out Type vs Cholesterol Level

* Pie Chart → Distribution of Eat-Out Types


## 🔍 Key Insights

* Frequent eating out (**5+ times/week**) leads to **higher BMI and lower health scores**

* **Fast food consumers** show higher cholesterol and health risks

* Individuals exercising **more than 4 hours/week** maintain better health

* **Students and employees** eat out more frequently

* Poor sleep and high calorie intake correlate with lower health scores


## 💡 Recommendations

* Limit eating out to **≤ 3 times per week**

* Increase physical activity to **4+ hours per week**

* Choose healthier options like **healthy cafés**

* Maintain balanced diet and sleep routine
