# Energy Consumption & Revenue Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing electricity consumption, billing revenue, and customer payment behavior in the energy domain using MySQL, Excel Power Query, and Power BI.

The objective is to identify energy consumption trends, monitor pending payments, analyze regional usage patterns, and generate business insights for better decision-making.

---

# Problem Statement
An energy company wants to optimize electricity distribution and improve profitability by analyzing:

- Energy consumption
- Revenue generation
- Payment status
- Customer behavior
- Regional demand trends
